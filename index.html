LOCATION

We are located in Jinja District in Buwenge Town Council in Bugoma Subcounty

At Joshua's Poultry Farm, we’re dedicated to providing the highest quality eggs and poultry products, raised with care and respect for both the animals and the environment. Our farm operates with sustainable practices to ensure that every product is not only fresh but also produced with the highest standards of animal welfare.

Why Choose Joshua's Poultry Farm?

Freshness Guaranteed: Our eggs and poultry products are laid and processed on-site to ensure the highest levels of freshness and taste.

Ethical Farming Practices: We believe in treating our animals with care and respect. Our chickens are free-range, fed with a balanced, nutritious diet, and raised in a clean and comfortable environment.

Sustainable and Eco-friendly: We use eco-conscious farming techniques that minimize our carbon footprint and make sure that our products contribute positively to the environment.

Local and Community-Focused: We’re proud to serve our local community with fresh, high-quality poultry products. By supporting Joshua's Poultry Farm, you’re supporting local agriculture.

Our Products

Fresh Eggs: Available in a range of sizes and colors, our eggs are perfect for baking, cooking, or just enjoying on their own.

Free-Range Chicken: Raised naturally with plenty of space to roam, our chickens are among the healthiest and tastiest you’ll find.

Specialty Items: From organic feed to farm-fresh chicken cuts, explore our full range of products for every need.
